# E-commerce Monthly Revenue Forecasting

## Project Goal
Predict monthly revenue trends for the next quarter to assist in financial planning and inventory management.

## Technical Stack
* **Forecasting Model:** Holt-Winters Exponential Smoothing.
* **Feature Engineering:** Integrated seasonal indicators such as `is_black_friday` and `quarterly_trends`.
* **Model Evaluation:** Achieved a **15.4% MAPE**, indicating high predictive accuracy for business operations.

## Key Insights
* Revenue shows a significant stabilization around 1M BRL after a period of rapid growth.
* The model correctly identified seasonal peaks, allowing for data-driven budget allocation during Q4.

## Business Value
This model enables a proactive approach to inventory management, reducing the risk of stockouts during peak seasons.
